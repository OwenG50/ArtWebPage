Our Artists

Anna Johnson

Anna is an artist from Dallas, TX. She specializes in sculpting, photography, and interactive media. Anna loves her two dogs, Milo and Bella and has many works about animals and her pets.

Bella Bruno

Bella is an artist from Santa Monica, CA. She specializes in painting using oils and pastels. Bella loves surfing, the outdoors, and baking. Bella is inspired by her mom who was the one who introduced her to art.

Anthony Brown

Anthony is an artist from Rosewood, CA. He specializes in abstract painting using acrylics and creating custom carpet and rug pieces. Anthony collaborates with his wife Amy to create these beautiful works.

Carlos Mendella

Carlos is an artist from Bradenton, FL. He specializes in photography and photoshop pieces. Carlos came to america with his family in 2002 and has been pursuing his art career ever since.

Vincent Grey

Vincent is an artist from Marietta, GA. He specializes in graffiti and multipurpose artwork. His work has been recognized by the NGAC (National Grafitti Art Committee) and has been featured in multiple magazines and online articles for his works.
